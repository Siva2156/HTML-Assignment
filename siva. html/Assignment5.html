My Favorite Movies

    Sita Ramam
      5 August 2022
      Dulquer Salmaan,Mrunal Thakur,Rashmika Mandanna,Sumanth,Bhoomika Chawla,Sachin Khedekar,Gautham Vasudev Menon.
      Sita Ramam released theatrically on 5 August 2022 to generally positive critical reviews praising Hanu's direction, story, screenplay, writing, soundtrack, musical score, emotional weight and performances (particularly those of Thakur and Salmaan). The film emerged as a major commercial success, grossing ₹91.4 crores (11 million USD) [a] at the box office, becoming the ninth highest-grossing Telugu film of 2022.
    Salaar
      22 December 2023
      Prabhas,Prithviraj Sukumaran,Shruti Haasan,Easwari Rao,Sriya Reddy,Jagapathi Babu,Bobby Simha.
      Salaar was theatrically released on 22 December 2023 in standard and IMAX formats to generally positive reviews from critics, with Neel's direction, story, world building, characterization, cast performances, action sequences, music, background scores, cinematography, technical aspects, and the realistic depiction of modern-day monarchy receiving praise. It was a commercial success, earning ₹715 crore on a ₹270 crore budget, making it the highest-grossing Telugu film of 2023, highest-grossing South Indian film of 2023, third highest-grossing Telugu film of all time, fourth highest grossing Indian film of 2023 and the second highest-grossing A-rated Indian film of all-time after the Hindi film Animal.
    Pushpa
      17 December 2021
      Allu Arjun,Fahadh Faasil,Rashmika Mandanna,Jagadeesh Prathap Bandari,Sunil,Sunil,Dhananjaya,Anasuya Bharadwaj .
      The Rise was released on 17 December 2021. The film received generally mixed reviews from critics, who praised the performances, action choreography, cinematography, direction, dialogues, and soundtrack but criticised the runtime, screenplay and editing. The film was commercially successful, grossing over ₹360–373 crore at the worldwide box office. It became the highest-grossing Indian film of 2021, and ranks among the highest-grossing Telugu films of all time. In 2024 it was featured retrospectively at the 74th Berlinale.
    The Family Star

      Vijay Deverakonda,Mrunal Thakur,Jagapathi Babu,Divyansha Kaushik,Vennela Kishore,Ravi Prakash,Rohini Hattangadi,Abhinaya.
      Family Star was initially scheduled to release on Sankranthi but it was postponed to avoid clash with Sankranthi films.[7] The Family Star received generally negative reviews from critics, who heavily criticised Parasuram's writing and direction, weak comedy, plot, predictability, soundtrack, and the lead cast's performances.     Premalu
      9 February 2024
      Naslen K. Gafoor,Mamitha Baiju,Sangeeth Prathap,Mathew Thomas,Althaf Salim.
      Premalu was released theatrically on February 9, 2024, to critical acclaim with praise for its performances, script, soundtrack, direction and humor.[7] It ranks as the sixth highest-grossing Malayalam film of all time and the fourth highest-grossing Malayalam film of 2024. A sequel, titled Premalu 2, is in development.
